My girlfriend's granddad passed away a couple of months ago. Her grandma is old and has been slowly losing her short term memory. Needless to say, she was extremely torn up about losing her husband of 60 years. To make matters worse, every morning when she wakes up, she forgets he's dead. She makes him breakfast and takes it in to the study. Doesn't find him there, so walks into every room in the house looking for him. Calls up one of her kids to ask where he is... They tell her what happened, and she spends the rest of her day crying like he just died that day.
